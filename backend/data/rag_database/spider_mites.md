### Extension Guidance: Spider Mite Management (Tetranychus urticae)
- Economic Threshold: Treat if fine webbing is visible on leaf undersides of >5% of plants.
- Recommended Dosage: Spray Abamectin at 0.5 ml per litre of water. Requires 150 litres of mix per acre.
- Preventive practices: Mitigate dry dusty road borders which attract mites by maintaining perimeter misting.
