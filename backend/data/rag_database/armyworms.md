### Extension Guidance: Fall Armyworm Management (Spodoptera frugiperda)
- Economic Threshold: Treat if crop damage is visible on >10% of corn plants.
- Recommended Dosage: Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt) at 2g per litre of water. Standard dilution is 150 litres per acre.
- Stage-specific: Target early instar larvae with Bt or Spinosad. Chemical control is less effective on mature larvae hiding inside whorls.
