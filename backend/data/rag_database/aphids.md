### Extension Guidance: Aphid Management (Aphis gossypii)
- Economic Threshold: Treat if more than 20 aphids per leaf are counted.
- Recommended Dosage: Apply Neem Oil at a rate of 5 ml per litre of water. Typically requires 2 litres of diluted spray mix per 10 square meters (approx 200 litres per acre).
- Larvae stage treatment: Ladybugs release is highly effective. Avoid pyrethroid sprays to protect beneficial ladybugs.
