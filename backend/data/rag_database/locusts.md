### Extension Guidance: Desert Locust Management (Schistocerca gregaria)
- Economic Threshold: Treat immediately if hopping bands or swarms are sighted.
- Recommended Dosage: Use Metarhizium acridum biological pathogen at 50g per hectare, or Lambda-cyhalothrin at 20 ml per acre dissolved in 100 litres of water.
- Stage-specific: Insect Growth Regulators (IGRs) like Diflubenzuron are highly recommended for immature hopper stages.
